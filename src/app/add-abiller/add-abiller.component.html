Auto Billpay Setup

Auto bill pay is supported by this biller for both Bank account and Credit card payment method

Important Information

1.To register multiple billers for a single category please use separate forms. Before registering a biller please check if the biller is on the list provided by the bank.

2.You can only register a biller with whom the Bank already has a tie-up. To find out the list of registered billers you can call-up the contact center, check-up the list of billers on www.hsbc.co.in or contact your nearest HSBC branch.

3.When you register for a biller on the ‘View and Pay’ service, you will be able to make payments to such billers through the ATM,PhoneBanking and Internet Banking. Registration for the service is not immediate and the time taken for registration varies from biller to biller. For detailed listing of registration time kindly refer to www.hsbc.co.in

4.Upon successful registration, you will be intimated on the E-mail ID provided by you in the form. For any queries, you can contact PhoneBanking or send a secured message through HSBC’s Internet Banking.
